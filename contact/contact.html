Contact

Have a question? Find something missing or incorrect? Interested in contributing? Just want to say hi? Send us a message via the contact form or plain old email.

 

Gilox Editors 2

If you’d like to get in touch with our editorial team use the contact form or email below. Note, we do not accept paid guest posts, link exchanges or sponsored articles. If you’re interested in advertising or partnership opportunities please include that in your email subject line or message body.

General Inquiries

Use the contact form below for any general inquires. If email is your preferred method of communication send a note to chuks@gilox.com.

Contributing

We're always on the lookout for talented writers and educators with an interest and/or background in the classics, ancient history, mythology, religious, museum and curatorial studies, and any other disciplines ancillary to our project.

If you’d like to contribute original work to one of the mythology sections (or propose one that does not yet exist) please include 2–3 references to written works (URLs are fine as long as you’re listed as the author) along with a resume or relavent experience/education in your message or email.
